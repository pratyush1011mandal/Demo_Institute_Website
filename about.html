About Aditi Institute of Excellence

Committed to Educational Excellence and Student Success

Our Mission

At Tatwamasi Foundation, our mission is to make quality education accessible to every deserving child and create opportunities for holistic growth through innovative and inclusive learning. Through our flagship institutions — Aditi Institute of Excellence and the upcoming Aditi VidyaNiketan School — we aim to nurture academic excellence, critical thinking, creativity, and strong moral character. We believe education is the foundation upon which all possibilities are built, and we are committed to shaping confident, future-ready individuals who can positively contribute to society.

Our Vision

Our vision is to build an inclusive educational ecosystem where no talent is left behind. We aspire to empower students, especially those facing barriers to formal schooling, by providing rigorous and meaningful learning opportunities in English, Mathematics, coding, and life skills. Through experiential and student-centered education, we envision a future where every child is equipped with the knowledge, confidence, and values needed to thrive in a rapidly evolving world.

Why We Stand Out

Experienced Faculty

Our teachers are highly qualified professionals with years of experience in their respective fields.

Comprehensive Curriculum

We offer well-structured courses designed to meet board requirements and competitive exam standards.

Student-Centric Approach

Individual attention and personalized learning plans ensure every student reaches their full potential.

Modern Facilities

Well-equipped classrooms, and learning spaces support an optimal educational experience.

Proven Track Record

Our students consistently achieve excellent results in board exams and competitive entrance tests.

Holistic Development

Beyond academics, we focus on developing communication skills, confidence, and character.

Our Commitment

We are committed to:

  • Providing accessible and quality education to deserving students
  • Ensuring no talented child is deprived of learning opportunities
  • Promoting academic excellence along with creativity and innovation
  • Building strong foundations in English, Mathematics, and coding
  • Encouraging critical thinking and problem-solving abilities
  • Creating a supportive and inclusive learning environment
  • Fostering character, confidence, and lifelong learning values
  • Preparing students to become responsible and future-ready citizens
